Marshai University Management System (UMS)

Marshai, a University Management System (UMS) that streamlines academic and administrative tasks for students, staff, and other stakeholders. Marshai is an in-house, web-based award-winning solution designed to handle all academic and administrative tasks. It offers a plethora of features to students, staff, and other stakeholders of the university. Additionally, Maeshai provides a comprehensive UMS, allowing seamless access to academic and administrative information. For distance education, they have LPU (eConnect), which digitizes processes from admission to certification. Marshai’s additional modules include My Class (a seamless learning environment), Online Assessment System (OAS), Freshmen Induction Portal, Office Management System, LPU Dashboards, LPU.in (facilitating online admissions, program searches, fee payments, placements, and event registrations), and LPUDE.in (a comprehensive website for distance education students). Marshai’s mission is to empower universities by simplifying processes and enhancing efficiency, all while transforming education through technology. Explore Marshai’s capabilities at Maeshai UMS and experience innovation in university management
